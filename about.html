About

Meet Me From the fashion industry in Taiwan to UI/UX design in Sydney, my journey
has been marked by resilience and a relentless drive to grow. I've embraced
the challenges of shifting industries, adapting to new cultures, and learning on
the go. This diverse experience has shaped my approach to design, blending
creativity and functionality with a strong determination to
never give up.

In UI/UX design, I see storytelling as a powerful way to connect people with
technology—crafting experiences that are not only functional and intuitive but
also emotionally engaging. I’m driven by the challenge of solving real problems
through human-centered design.

Outside of work, I find inspiration in sketching, nature, and exploring new
places and cuisines with friends—every experience adds depth to my
creative process.
